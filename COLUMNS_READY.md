## 🚀 Create the Form (2 Minutes)

Now that columns are ready, create the form:

### Step 1: Add Form View
1. Click **"+ Add View"** (top of board, next to "Main table")
2. Select **"Form"**
3. Name it: "Wellness Feedback Form"

### Step 2: Add Questions
The form builder will show all your columns. Select these:

**Question 1: Name** (optional)
- Toggle OFF "Required"

**Question 2: Email** (optional)  
- Toggle OFF "Required"

**Question 3: Feedback** (required)
- Toggle ON "Required" ✅

**Question 4: Category** (optional)
- Toggle OFF "Required"

### Step 3: Get Form URL
1. Click **"Share"** button
2. Copy the **Public link**
3. Save it - looks like: `https://forms.monday.com/forms/abc123xyz`

### Step 4: Update index.html
Replace `YOUR_MONDAY_FORM_URL_HERE` with your form URL (line ~330)
